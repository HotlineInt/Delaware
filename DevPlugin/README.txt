DevPlugin - a development suite for mappers and others

Included features:
 EDITORS:
    Node Editor - change node properties with ease
    Weapon Editor - create or edit weapons with ease
 PREVIEWERS:
    CUI Previewer - realtime editing of CUI components
    ViewModel Previewer - helps with animating and editing viewmodels,
 HELPERS:
    Optimization Helper - shows you what could be enabled or disabled to help get the most fps out of your game 
 MISC:
    Light and Everything Else Gizmos - shows lights, billboards and etc

DEPENDENCIES:
    Carbon v0.0.1 and above, no lower (no support for dev versions outside of internal work)
